Inspiration Behind the Project

The inspiration for this project sprouted from my love for Club House, a popular social application and my belief in the power of real-time communication. Having used the app during the covid-19 global lock-down, I was fascinated about how it works and technology bedind it. With this inspiration and the opportunity the final project presented, I was inspired to try to create my own real time chat application. I recognize the need for a platform that not only facilitates instant messaging but also prioritizes user experience and accessibility. Whether you're connecting with friends, family collaborating on projects, or meeting new people, I wanted to create a space where conversations flow effortlessly.
